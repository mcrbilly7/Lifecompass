    Frequently Asked Questions

    • Is the free version actually enough?

      Yes. You get unlimited goals, unlimited steps, daily planning and insights. You can use Life Compass forever without paying anything.

    • Where is my data stored?

      Everything is stored locally in your browser using private local storage. It’s not uploaded to a cloud database by default. If you clear your browser data, you can lose it – that’s why we added the backup download button.

    • What do I actually get with Premium?

      Premium gives you a real human on your side. You’ll get a weekly review of your goals, a customized weekly plan, and email check–ins that keep you moving when you feel stuck.

    • Is Life Compass therapy or medical advice?

      No. Life Compass is a gentle planning and accountability tool. It’s not a replacement for therapy, counseling, or medical care. If you’re in crisis, please reach out to local emergency services or a trusted professional.

    • How do I cancel Premium?

      Just reply to any Premium email or contact lifecompass270@gmail.com and ask to cancel. No tricks, no hidden steps.
