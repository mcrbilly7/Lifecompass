Free first. Helpful always.

Stop trying to fix your whole life at once.

Life Compass breaks big goals into tiny, honest steps – and quietly learns how you work so it can recommend realistic next moves.

Free forever. No accounts required. Create one later if you want Life Compass to remember you across devices.

No fake productivity Works with “messy” days Built for real life, not perfect people

Short term

Use “Today” to pick 3–7 small steps that would make the day feel like a win.

  • Clean one small area, not the whole room
  • Send one message you’ve been avoiding
  • Open the homework and do 5 minutes

Long term

Create gentle long–term goals. Life Compass will pick soft due dates for you.

  • Save your first $100 emergency buffer
  • Raise one grade by a single letter
  • Build a basic workout or sleep routine

Accounts (optional)

Use it as a guest forever, or create a free account so it can learn your patterns and give better recommendations.

In a future version, accounts will sync across devices and unlock deeper personalization. Right now everything is saved on your device.
